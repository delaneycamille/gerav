Capture your excitement and anticipation of your little one, as well as your body at its most feminine. There’s a $150 discount on the maternity session package price of $1299 when you also book a newborn session at the same time (priced separately – newborn session package and pricing are below).

Products and Add-Ons-

I offer the following professionally-designed and printed photographic products:

  • High-quality prints – color-calibrated, printed on lustre paper, and mounted on sturdy art board to preserve and extend the life of the photograph.  Prints start at $49 for a 5×7 and $69 for an 8×10.
